What to Demo

With the new data, you can showcase the following features:

  • Multi-Store & Tenant Support: Show how the system can manage different stores and tenants. The "Durban Spice Grill" is on a separate tenant.
  • Comprehensive Menu: Browse the menu with local dishes like "Boerewors Roll" and "Melktert."
  • Inventory Management: Go to the inventory section to see stock levels for ingredients like Boerewors and Pap, and view a completed purchase order.
  • HR & Payroll: Demonstrate the employee management, shift scheduling, and payroll features. You can see how shifts are logged and payroll is calculated.
  • Order Lifecycle: View orders with different statuses (e.g., a "completed" dine-in order and a "pending" takeaway order).
